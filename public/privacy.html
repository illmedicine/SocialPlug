Privacy Policy

SocialPlug · by Illy Robotic Instruments

Effective date: May 11, 2026
Application: SocialPlug (web at illmedicine.github.io/SocialPlug and the Android app, package com.illyroboticinstruments.socialplug)
Operator: Illy Robotic Instruments ("we", "us", "our")

1. Overview

SocialPlug is a cloud virtual-machine management and social-automation tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We designed SocialPlug to keep your data inside infrastructure you control (Firebase project and cloud VMs that you provision) — we do not operate a separate analytics or advertising platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account information (from Google Sign-In)

When you sign in with Google, we receive the basic profile information you authorize Google to share: your Google account display name, email address, profile photo URL, and a unique account identifier (uid). We do not receive your Google password.

2.2 Application data you create

  • Virtual-machine configuration (name, specs, provider, agent key, status).
  • Workflow definitions, blocks, and platform credentials you choose to store.
  • User preferences (screenshot interval, max tabs per VM, theme).
  • Browser screenshots captured by VM agents you run, plus session metadata.

2.3 Technical / operational data

  • VM heartbeat data (timestamp, public IP, agent version).
  • Cloud Function invocation metadata (timestamps, error codes).
  • Standard server logs from Firebase Hosting / GitHub Pages (IP address, user agent, request URL) — retained per the respective providers' policies.

We do not collect: contacts, SMS, call logs, microphone audio, camera images outside of VM screenshots that you initiate, precise location, or biometrics. The Android app does not include any advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

3. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate you and associate data with your account.
  • To render your dashboard, VM list, workflows, and screenshots.
  • To execute the VM lifecycle actions you trigger (start, stop, restart, delete).
  • To diagnose errors and improve reliability.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with advertisers.

4. Where Data Is Stored

  • Authentication, Firestore database, Storage, Cloud Functions: Google Firebase, project livepay-petition. Same backend whether you use the web app or the Android app.
  • Virtual machines: infrastructure you provision in Microsoft Azure and/or Oracle Cloud, under your tenant credentials. Screenshots captured on those VMs are uploaded to your Firestore.
  • Static web hosting: GitHub Pages.

These third parties process data on our and your behalf and have their own privacy policies: Firebase, Google, Microsoft Azure, Oracle, GitHub.

5. Permissions Used by the Android App

  • Internet — required to talk to Firebase and your VMs.
  • Network state — to detect offline conditions.
  • Sign in with Google — to authenticate you using your existing Google account via the standard Google Sign-In flow.

The app requests no other sensitive runtime permissions.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and associated data at any time from the in-app Settings page, or by emailing us (see Section 10). Deletion removes your user document, VM documents, workflows, screenshots, and revokes your Google Sign-In session. Backups in Firebase may persist for up to 30 days before being purged.

To request deletion without installing the app, email privacy@illyroboticinstruments.com from the Google account you used to sign in. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

7. Security

All traffic between SocialPlug and Firebase uses HTTPS. Firestore is protected by security rules that allow each user to access only documents associated with their own uid. VM agent credentials are stored encrypted at rest by Firebase. We follow industry best practices but no system is perfectly secure; please use a strong Google account password and enable two-factor authentication on your Google account.

8. Children

SocialPlug is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete personal information we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us at the address in Section 10 to exercise these rights.
